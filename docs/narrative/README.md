# Narrative & World-Building

This folder contains ideas about story, characters, setting, and the world of our game.

## What Goes Here?

- Story concepts and plot ideas
- Character designs and backgrounds
- World-building and lore
- Setting descriptions
- Narrative themes
- Dialogue or writing samples
- Quest/mission ideas

## Template for Narrative Ideas

When creating a new narrative document, consider including:

```markdown
# [Story/Character/World Element Name]

## Concept
What is this about?

## Details
The meat of your idea - describe it!

## How It Fits
How does this connect to gameplay or other story elements?

## Mood/Tone
What feeling does this create?

## Inspiration
What inspired this? (games, books, movies, etc.)

## Notes
Additional thoughts
```

## Examples to Get Started

- Main character background
- World setting (fantasy, sci-fi, modern, etc.)
- Central conflict or story hook
- Supporting characters
- Factions or groups
- Key locations
- Backstory and lore
