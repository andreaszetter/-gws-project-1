## What You'll Be Asked

The form has simple questions about your idea:

1. **Your Name** (optional) - You can contribute anonymously!
2. **Idea Title** - Give your idea a catchy name
3. **Core Concept** - Briefly describe your game idea
4. **Category** - Choose what type of idea it is (mechanic, story, technical, etc.)
5. **Detailed Description** - Add more details if you want
6. **Genre/Type** - What kind of game? (RPG, Puzzle, etc.)
7. **Inspiration** - What inspired this idea?
8. **Excitement Level** - How excited are you about this? (1-5)
9. **Additional Comments** - Anything else to add?

Don't worry - only the first 3 questions are required! Share as much or as little as you want.

## What Happens Next?

1. **You submit** - Takes just a few minutes
2. **We review** - A team member reviews your submission
3. **We add it** - Your idea gets added to the repository within a few days
4. **You get credit** - If you provided your name, we'll credit you!
